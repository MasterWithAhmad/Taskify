Frequently Asked Questions
How do I create a new task?
Click the "Add Task" button on the dashboard or calendar page. Fill in the task details including title, description, due date, priority, and status. Click "Save" to create the task.
How do I organize tasks into projects?
Go to the Projects page and click "Add Project". Once created, you can assign tasks to projects by editing the task and selecting the project from the dropdown menu.
How do I set task reminders?
Enable task reminders in your notification settings. You can then set reminder times when creating or editing tasks. You'll receive notifications via email or browser notifications.
How do I generate reports?
Visit the Reports page to view task statistics, completion trends, and performance metrics. You can filter reports by date range and export them if needed.
